FIND US

Institute of Management and Information Technology known as IMIT, is located at Gourav Palace, Matha sahi, Post-Tulsipur in Cuttack, district of Odisha, India. The college is well connected by both rail and road networks, making it easily accessible to visitors and students. If you're traveling by train, the nearest railway station to IMIT is the Cuttack railway station, which is approximately 6 kilometers away. From the railway station, you can either hire a taxi or take a bus to reach the college. If you're traveling by bus, the nearest bus stand to IMIT is the Badambadi bus stand, which is located around 5-6 kilometer away from the college. From the bus stand, you can either take MoBus or take a rickshaw, to reach the college.
