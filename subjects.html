OUR FACULTIES

Our faculties are one of the most important people of our branch.Because of their teaching and guidence the branch has gone this far and became one of the most successful branch of our college.

Most of the faculty members have received advanced degrees and/or training from reputed institutions in India. Many alumni have been employed in reputed public sector undertakings and academic institutions in India. The department has laboratory facilties with well equipped Linux lab, Windows lab, Microprocessor lab. Our mission is to produce Best Quality Computer Science Professionals by imparting quality training, hands on experiences and value education,To attain self-sustainability and overall development through Research, Consultancy and Development Activities, To extend technical expertise to other technical Institutions of the region and play a lead role in imparting education in computer science and information technology.
